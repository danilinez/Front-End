Negotiate the right price

You might not like to haggle, but it’s what we do every day. When you’re ready to buy, we will negotiate with at least three competitive dealers on your behalf.

When the offers are ready, we’ll let you know the important details, like the dealer location, the expected delivery date and what’s included in the price. You choose the deal that best suits you.
