Fleet Plan Privacy Policy

The FleetPlan Austraila Privacy Policy ("Privacy Policy") sets out:

  • - why we need to collect personal information;
  • - how we collect it;
  • - what we do with it;
  • - how it's stored; and;
  • - who we might share it with and why.

The Privacy Policy also describes:

  • - how you can access or correct information we hold about you;
  • - how you can ask further questions or make a complaint; and
  • - information about our websites and online activities.

Personal Information

'Personal information' is defined within The Privacy Act (1988) as any "information or an opinion, whether true or not, and whether recorded in a material form or not, about an identified individual, or an individual who is reasonably identifiable."

The Privacy Act (1988).

Fleet Plan is bound by the Privacy Act (1988).

The information we collect and why

In providing you with the FleetPlan Australia Applications and Services ("FleetPlan Australia") we will occasionally receive, store and process 'personal information' about you that you make available to us.

The 'Personal information' we may collect from you includes (but is not limited to):


  • Your name;
  • Your email address;
  • Your phone number;
  • Your postcode;
  • Your devices IP Adress;

Other information (that is not necessarily 'personal information') we may collect about you during your use of FleetPlan Australia includes (but is not limited to): your car preferences, your car buying budget, your car buying timeframe, information about your car trade in and whether or not you are seeking finance.

Specific times you will provide personal information to us include (but are not limited to):


  • When you view drive away pricing, which is based on your location;
  • When you create an account with FleetPlan Australia;
  • When you request FleetPlan Australia assistance in purchasing a car (also referred to as "getting a quote");
  • When you request a test drive of a car;
  • When you communicate with FleetPlan Australia in person, phone calls, emails and via social media;
  • When you link your account on a third party site (e.g. Facebook) to your FleetPlan Australia account, in which case we will obtain the Personal Information that you have provided to the third party site, to the extent allowed by your settings with the third party site and authorised by you.

Without such information, we may not be able to provide you with an appropriate level of service.

How we store and secure your information

Keeping your information secure

We use security procedures and technology to protect the information we hold. Access to and use of personal information within FleetPlan Australia seeks to prevent misuse or unlawful disclosure of the information, this includes internal policies, auditing, training and monitoring of staff.


If other organisations provide support services, we require them to appropriately safeguard the privacy of the information provided to them.

How we use your information

We use personal information about you for the purpose for which it was provided to us, including:

  • To provide you with drive away pricing on the cars you browse;
  • To create a FleetPlan Australia account for you and store your information and preferences;
  • To personalise your experience with FleetPlan Australia based on your information and preferences;
  • To assist you with purchasing a car (also referred to as "getting a quote");
  • To assist you in arranging a test drive of a car;
  • To communicate with you and deal with or investigate any complaints or enquiries.

We may also use information you provide – de-identified wherever possible – to:

  • administer, manage, improve and optimise FleetPlan Australia (including monitoring, auditing, and evaluating FleetPlan Australia);
  • model and test data (in a controlled environment);
  • develop scores, risk assessments and related analytical tools;
  • conduct market research, new product development and fraud mitigation;
  • undertake related purposes which would reasonably be expected without your express permission. For example, we may from time to time use your personal information to inform you of products and services which we expect may be of interest to you. However, if you do not want to receive such communications you can tell us:
    • At the time of collection of your personal information;
    • By following the instructions to 'unsubscribe' or 'opt-out' which will be included in any such communications sent to you, which may include accessing a web-link or replying to a message;
    • By writing to us at enquiries@fleetplan.com.au requesting that you be unsubscribed from such communications.

While we may sometimes – where it is lawful and with your permission if necessary – share personal information with companies we do business with (e.g. in product development, joint venture arrangements or distribution arrangements), we do not sell personal information for marketing purposes to other organisations or allow such companies to do this.

When your information is disclosed

We may share personal information within FleetPlan Australia and, where necessary to provide the requested service to you, may disclose your personal information outside FleetPlan Australia in the following situations:

Requesting a test drive

When you request a test drive via FleetPlan Australia, we must necessarily disclose the information you provide to the car dealer who will fulfil your test drive request. Any information expressly provided by you when you request a test drive may be passed on to the car dealer, including your preference as to whether or not you would like to receive promotional communications from the car dealer; by default, you will be opted-out of receiving such communications.

Requesting assistance buying a car

When you request assistance purchasing a car (also referred to as "getting a quote") via FleetPlan Australia, we must necessarily disclose the information you provide to the car dealer who you select to purchase the car from. Any information expressly provided by you when you request assistance purchasing a car may be passed on to this car dealer, including your preference as to whether or not you would like to receive promotional communications from the car dealer; by default, you will be opted-out of receiving such communications.

Other:

We may also share personal information within FleetPlan Australia, and may disclose personal information outside FleetPlan Australia as required by law or regulations.

Dealing with FleetPlan Australia online

When you visit FleetPlan Australia websites you will browse anonymously unless you have logged into your FleetPlan Australia account or accessed the website from a personalised communication from FleetPlan Australia.

For all visitors to our web site, we use cookies and/or browser local storage to collect anonymous information such as the server your computer is logged on to, your browser type (for example, Internet Explorer, Chrome or Firefox), and your IP address. (An IP address is a number that is assigned to your computer automatically and required for using the Internet). We may also derive the location associated with an IP address.

Once you have logged into your FleetPlan Australia account, accessed our website from an e-mail or other personalised communication sent to you, or provided us with personal information by completing a form online, we may be able to identify you and we may combine that with other information in order to provide you with a better online experience. If you would prefer not to be identified you can delete the cookies and/or local storage data and reconfigure the tracking preferences on your internet browser (see the 'Cookies and local storage' section below).

While you may disable tracking through your browser settings, we do not change our practices in response to a "Do Not Track" signal in the HTTP header from your browser or mobile application. We track your activities if you click on advertisements for FleetPlan Australia services on third party platforms such as search engines and social networks, and may use analytics to track what you do in response to those advertisements.

Cookies and local storage

A 'cookie' is a packet of information placed on a user's computer by a website for record keeping purposes.

'Local storage' is a standard facility provided by HTML5-compliant browsers used to retain data on your computer across visits to the same website.

While cookies and local storage are not considered personal information, they are generally used on FleetPlan Australia sites to:


  • Access online services - if you log into secure areas on our website we will use cookies and/or local storage to authorise your access and save your preferences;
  • Manage advertising - when you see one of our ads on a third party website, cookies are sometimes used to collect anonymous information about the page you visit and the type of software you are using;
  • Monitor traffic - we use anonymous information to track how people are using the FleetPlan Australia site. This may include time of visit, pages visited and some system information about the type of computer you are using;
  • Monitor your use of our website - if we have identified you as a result of you logging into a FleetPlan Australia client portal, accessing our website from a personalised communication or providing us with personal information by completing a form online, we may use cookies and/or local storage to collect personal information about you.

You can configure how your browser stores cookies and local storage data, which may include the ability to accept, reject or notify you about attempts to store this data (Each browser is different, so check the "Help" menu of your browser to learn how to change your cookie and site data preferences).

If you disable the use of cookies and/or local storage on your web browser or remove or reject specific cookies and/or local storage from FleetPlan Australia or linked sites, then you may not be able to gain access to all of the content and features on those websites.
