WHC Affiliate Programs

Associate yourself with one of the fastest growing healthcare company. With a network of more than 5000 physicians and health care professionals, 1200 dentists, 100 hospitals and a network of retail pharmacies, DME suppliers and manufacturers , WHC offers something for every type of healthcare professional.

The WHC Affiliate Program provides many ways to differentiate your business and drive growth and profitability for your company. As a WHC affiliate you can earn commissions on any product sale using your personal business website or one of our affiliated partners. This is a unique combination. Becoming our affiliate is absolutely free and a great way to earn commissions each time any of your site visitors click on WHC product and service' section. We offer you the solutions, training, tools and support to set you and your customer apart from the competition. All you have to do is click on WHC’s "product and service".

1. E-Commerce Affiliate Program

As an E-Commerce Affiliate you can earn commissions on any product sale using your personal business website. Becoming our affiliate is completely free and is also a great way to earn commissions each time any of your site visitors click on WHC’s "product and service" section.

We offer you the solutions, training, tools and support to set you and your customers apart from the competition.

2. Venture Capital Partnership Program

WHC also offers a Venture Capital Partnership that invests in healthcare companies with global ambitions. We invest across all stages, from startups to declining companies. Our portfolio of companies spans across three continents. Using the expertise of our business development consultants, we would design the right operational and marketing strategies to steer your business to success. We also work with entrepreneurs and other investors in the public and private sectors to build outstanding companies, and are involved at every stage of the development cycle, from seed through expansion.

At WHC, we help great ideas become great companies. Our goal is to deliver excellent return on our investments, while working to create a sound financial ecosystem for all healthcare ventures.

3. Corporate Partnership Program

WHC Corporate Partnership program offers select companies the distinctive opportunity to build special relationships with our current network of Partners and Affiliates. The Corporate Partnership Program, started in 2008, has grown from a group of contacts to the present day where the diversity of companies has increased significantly. Current partners operate in sectors as diverse as home health care, equipment manufacturers, technology, as well as pharmaceuticals retailers and distributors.

We have three levels of partnerships – Gold, Silver and Partner. The level of partnership depends on what your business can offer in sales volume, customer engagement and capabilities. The WHC Partner Program will recognize your expertise in giving customers the best possible service. We reward you for your impact in the marketplace and offer you a wide range of support and resources to help your business grow.
