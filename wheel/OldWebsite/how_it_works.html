How it works

For Patients

  1. Contact your Personal Account Manager first  at 1.855.WHEELHC (943.3542) or visit your family doctor
  2. From setting up your doctor’s appointment to delivery of your medicine/product to your doorstep.... , We can do it All!!!!

Prescription Transfer

Transferring your prescription is fast and easy, we take care of most of it for you! Create a free account today. An online account makes it easy for us to transfer your prescriptions/product to one of our partner Pharmacies

Start enjoying our free services and features like automatic refills, refill reminders and the convenience of ordering refills on-the-go with your mobile device. Plus, no matter which online refill method you use, you always have the option to pick up your prescription/product or have it shipped to you for FREE.

Please note: Due to state regulations, some prescriptions cannot be transferred between pharmacies. A WHC pharmacist will contact you if there are any issues. Please allow one extra business day for your order while the pharmacist attempts to contact your pharmacy.

Worker's Compensation

We accept most workers' compensation insurances. You will be asked to provide the following information prior to placing your order:

  • Employer Information
  • Workers' Compensation Carrier Name
  • Claim Number
  • Date of Injury/Accident
  • Adjuster's Contact Information (Name and Phone Number)












Please inform our staff if your insurance or personal information (address, phone, etc.) has recently changed. It is important that we have this information for insurance verification and pre-certification.

Credit Cards and Online Payments

WHC currently accepts Visa and MasterCard. You can safely enter your entire credit card number via our secure server, which encrypts all submitted information. Your card number will be protected using state-of-the-art encryption methods to assure a safe purchasing experience on our site.
