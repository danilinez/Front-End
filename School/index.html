About Us

Welcome to Cosvita School

Growing up in one of the poorest villages in Chad, Esaie Toingar considers himself to be very blessed to have come to the USA as a refugee after many years of turmoil and war in Chad, Africa. Esaie and his wife Brigitte have a burning desire to transform their bad experiences and struggles into a God honoring legacy by providing a different path to the new generation of youth in Chad. They hope to do this through the construction of a school. The school will help youth from varying backgrounds (e.g., Christians, Muslims, and Non-believers) to grow up together with a primary focus on developing a spirit of forgiveness and love.
