Product Information

Product Type : Bags - Economy Backpacks

The Infinite Stealth Bag is the ideal choice for disc golfers who like to travel light, play mountain courses, or run while playing a round.

Stealth Bag Features:

  • Comfortably holds 4-8 discs
  • Zip up pockets to hold phone, keys, and snacks
  • Ultra comfortable padding on back and straps
  • Chest and belt straps
  • Clips to hold towels and bag tags
  • Water bottle holder
  • Full size pocket that holds 2 liter water bladder

My Flight Ratings 12/5/-1/2.5 Not Beginner Friendly

This bag is great for what it is. Small bag for light game days. It will comfortably fit 2 discs in each slot (8 total) and you'll be able to draw each one out easily. Currently I've got 1 mid, 3 putters, and 4 drivers in mine. Personally I've found that stacking them either mid/driver or putter/driver works better than stacking 2 of the same kind together.
