Get connected with local blood donors

Raise a blood request among our volunteer blood donors. Our heroes within your area will be notified about your blood need. Blood donation is a voluntary procedure that can help save the lives of others. Donating blood is easy and our blood donation system relies exclusively on the generosity of volunteer blood donors. There is no substitute for human blood.

Our services are free of cost, we just connect you with the blood donor and do not take any responsibility.
Note: It is your duty to verify their blood before the transfusion.

Note: Blood requests can only be posted through our mobile app, please download to continue.
