APPLICATION PROCESS

Security excellence means top quality people, quality training and close supervision. Excellence also means a wide variety of additional services.

EMPHASIS ON SELECTION

The qualities that produce excellence in security must come from the people who provide that service. Our uncompromising selection procedures have a proven record of identifying those individuals who will please our clients with their performance.

Security Watch Inc places strong emphasis on its selection procedures and requires uniform compliance. Every person who becomes a SOI Security Watch Inc security officer has been thoroughly interviewed and investigated before receiving final approval from our personnel department.

DETAILED APPLICATION

The Security Watch.Inc employment application covers the past 10 years in the applicant's employment and residence histories, as well as a complete life medical history. A notarized explanation is required for any gap of 30 days or more in employment history.

MEDICAL HISTORY

Every applicant must submit a complete life medical history, detailing any past illnesses or health problems. Applicants whose medical histories indicate physical problems that would impede their performance are excluded from consideration.

EDUCATIONAL REQUIRMENTS

Well educated security officers are better able to perform with excellence in today's complex security environment. Security Watch Inc has one of the best educated work forces in the industry.

To ensure the high caliber of all security personnel, a high school diploma or equivalency certificate is required as a prerequisite to employment. However, individual exceptions may be considered, depending upon background and life experience.

HONORABLE DISCHARGE

No veteran with a discharge under less than honorable conditions is ever considered for employment.

LOCAL COMPLIANCE

Security Watch.Inc Security complies fully with all local, state and federal requirements concerning selection, training and licensing. However Security Watch requirements usually are more stringent than the local ones.

CRIMINAL RECORD CHECK

We check country court level records for any felony convictions and, when available, significant misdemeanors.

CONSUMER CREDIT REPORT

We use this report to verify an applicant's name and address, as well as to review the applicant's credit history.

SOCIAL SECURITY NUMBER VALIDATION

This check confirms an applicant's identity and the validity of issuance.

DRIVER LICENSE RECORD CHECK

We use this report to verify an applicant's identity, physical description, residence, and to screen for serious driving violations, such as driving under the influence.

WHO WE DO NOT EMPLOY

Safety Watch does not employ security officers with physical conditions that would prevent them from providing you with an excellent level of security services, such as heart disease, back problems, hearing loss, nervous conditions, serious emotional problems, and alcohol or drug problems. Safety Watch also refuses to hire those with bad employment histories, criminal records, and any other questionable backgrounds.
