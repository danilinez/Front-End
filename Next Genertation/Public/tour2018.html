WHAT IS THE NEXT GEN TOUR?

The Next Gen Tour is a far reaching network of professionally run tournaments that offer community, big prizes, and lifelong memories for every level of amateur disc golfer. We provide diverse benchmarks and a series of milestones to foster sportsmanship, personal growth, and the spirit of the game. Whether you are an aspiring professional disc golfer or just want to hone your skills, Next Generation Disc Golf will accelerate your game and take you to the next level.

NEXT GEN TOURNAMENTS

NEXT GEN

2019

Qualifier Tournament

The Next Gen C-Tiers are fun entry level tournaments for all levels of Disc Golf. They are all PDGA sanctioned, however, you don’t need to be a registered PDGA member to participate (PDGA fee is waived). There are 3 ratings based divisions creating a competitive environment for beginners all the way up to amateurs aspiring to become professionals.

NEXT GEN

2019

Qualifier Tournament

Each Premier Event will be a PDGA sanctioned B-Tier run by Dave and Synthya with player’s packs valued at over the $150 entry fee ($100 entry for qualifier participants) and over $28,000 in added prizes across the country. Dave, arguably one of the best teachers of the sport, will also provide a world class putting clinic and finals will be filmed, so you can be a YouTube star like the pros. The top 3 performances will punch their ticket to the prestigious National Championship.

Virtual Challange

If you would like to compete on a national level from the comfort of your local course try the Next Gen Virtual Challenge when you register for a Next Gen C-Tier Tournament. The average PDGA ratings of both qualifier rounds will be ranked against all other Virtual Challenge competitors in your division. Play locally and see how you rank nationally.

Double Down

Elect to enter your average rating for ANY upcoming PDGA sanctioned tournament where you play in an amateur division held prior to October 28, 2018 into the Regional Rankings. Learn more here.

REGIONAL VIRTUAL PAYOUTS

After the end of the Qualifier season Next Gen Regional Rankings will be finalized with $21,000 in prizes, sponsored by the Disc Golf Center, plus any contributions from the Double Down option, paid nationally to the top 12 ranked competitors in each division. See the current results here.
