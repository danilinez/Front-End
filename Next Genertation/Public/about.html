About

Next Generation Disc Golf is the official resource and disc golf tour for amateur disc golfers. Our far reaching network of professionally run tournaments offer community, big prizes, and lifelong memories for every level of amateur disc golfer. We provide diverse benchmarks and a series of milestones to foster sportsmanship, personal growth, and the spirit of the game. Whether you are an aspiring professional disc golfer or just want to hone your skills, Next Generation Disc Golf will accelerate your game and take you to the next level. The Next Generation Disc Golf movement is the vision of Synthya Whitaker and David Feldberg, partnered with Pat Govang who serves as architect and business director, and is realized through a dedicated core staff and nationwide team of Regional Directors, Coordinators and Tournament Directors – all committed to providing amateurs unparalleled tournament experiences and opportunity.
