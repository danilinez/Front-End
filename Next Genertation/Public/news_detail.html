SYNERGY ART BRIGHTENS NEXT GEN TOUR

November 13, 2018

Our Associate Director Synthya Feldberg does much more than help the Next Gen Tour run smoothly. She is a multitalented artist that has also provided countless stunning designs that can be seen throughout the tour. Through her company, Synergy Art, Synthia has designed trophies, disc art, and even a designed that was featured on a special edition Zuca cart. We are so grateful to have someone like Synthya to provide original designs that add another level of uniqueness to this one of a kind tour.
