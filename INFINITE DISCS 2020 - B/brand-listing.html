About Discmania

Discmania offers some of the best disc golf discs on the market. They have headquarters in Colorado, but work closely with Innova, Latitude 64 and Yikun to produce and manufacture their discs with their own unique designs. Discmania Originals are produced by Innova Champion Discs in California. The Discmania Evolution Line is produced by Latitude 64 in Sweden. The Discmania Active Line is produced by Yikun Discs in China.
