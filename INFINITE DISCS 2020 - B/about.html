About Infinite Discs

Infinite Discs was founded in 2012 with an emphasis on providing quality information and the best online disc golf experience.  

Our website features pictures of every disc, uniform flight characteristics, reviews by actual disc golfers, and an innovative online disc search tool. We are always looking to improve the site and help grow disc golf. In our short business life we have rapidly grown into one of the top online disc golf retailers, offering thousands of disc models from more than 35 different brands.

While our local shop and headquarters are located in Logan, Utah, we ship discs to all 50 states and many countries throughout the world.

In addition to an incredible selection of inventory, we also offer low every day prices and fast, same day processing. We also provide a free mobile score card app, online disc golf course directory, and opportunity to raise funds by hosting Infinite Tournaments. Annually we run the State of Disc Golf Survey and use data to provide information that helps tournament directors, parks and rec departments, and disc golf media to better grow the sport. Our regularly updated blog provides interesting articles, tips, and useful information regarding new releases and disc golf trends.

Thanks for using our online disc golf resources and shopping with Infinite Discs!
