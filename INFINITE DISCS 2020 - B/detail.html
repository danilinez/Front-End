Aztech

Rate this Disc

The Aztec is a speed 10 driver, flying staight with a little bit of fade at the end. Don't let the speed 10 fool you, the Aztec will bring unexpected distance and accuracy to your game. This disc has a skinnier rim than the big distance drivers, making it more comfortable for most disc golfers. The Aztec is said to be a longer Exodus or Sphinx.
